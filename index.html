About

Wyatt Kopelman has shot Division I college athletics on a freelance basis for the past two years. His work has been featured on digital platforms of the Miami Hurricanes baseball program, and across those of the Manhattan Jaspers of the Metro Atlantic Athletic Conference. In May 2024, he completed a master's degree in journalism at the University of Miami.
